### Shaders functionnalities
#### Water shader 
- Reflection
- Refraction 
- Depth 
- Waves 

#### Sand shader 
- Wind trails 
- Texture visibility depending on the direction of the dune (a face more on the wind will have a more flat texture than the other side)

#### Sky shader
- Spheremap
- Simple gradient using vertex world position
